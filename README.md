# 🌸 Rare Flowers & Botanical Wonders Website

A beautiful, modern website dedicated to exploring the world's most extraordinary and rare flowers, featuring fascinating botanical facts and conservation information.

## 🌺 Features

### Featured Flowers
- **Corpse Flower** - The world's largest unbranched flower with a distinctive odor
- **Flying Duck Orchid** - A flower that perfectly mimics a duck in flight
- **Flying Eagle Orchid** - A master of deception that attracts wasps for pollination
- **Ghost Orchid** - An elusive, nearly translucent orchid from Florida and Cuba
- **Monkey Face Orchid** - A rare orchid from the Peruvian Amazon that resembles a monkey's face
- **Anthurium (Flamingo Flower)** - A striking tropical flower with waxy, heart-shaped leaves
- **Bee Orchid** - A flower that tricks bees through visual and chemical mimicry
- **Moonflower** - A spectacular nocturnal flower that blooms only at night
- **Welwitschia** - A living fossil over 2,000 years old from the Namib Desert

### Fun Botanical Facts
- Explosive seed dispersal mechanisms
- Bioluminescent blooms
- Ancient seeds preserved in permafrost
- How flowers "hear" bee vibrations
- The rarity of true purple flowers
- And many more fascinating botanical discoveries!

### Conservation Section
Information about protecting rare flowers and what you can do to help preserve these botanical treasures.
